Risk Architecture

Systematic Risk Controls

Institutional-grade risk management built into every layer. Automated, regime-aware, and mathematically rigorous.

Kelly Criterion Sizing

Mathematically optimal position sizing, regime-scaled. Full Kelly (~16.8%) in Bull, quarter-Kelly in Panic. Hard-capped at 8% per position to bound concentration.

Convexity Overlay

QQQ protective puts — 30-45 DTE far OTM puts, 1.5% allocation in normal regimes, 3.5% in stress. Defined-risk tail protection with convex crash payoff and max drawdown under 7%.

VRP Circuit Breaker

Automatic exit on VIX breach above 40. Prevents vol-selling exposure during extreme dislocations where short-volatility strategies face catastrophic risk.

Trend-Following Exits

Death cross signal caps QQQ allocation to 20%. Early warning reduces to 50%. Net effect: QQQ floor max drawdown compressed from 30.9% to 17.7%.

Regime-Aware Everything

Every parameter is regime-conditional. Allocation, sizing, entry thresholds, stop distances, and hedge budgets all shift across Bull/Neutral/Caution/Panic states.

Geometric Compounding

Kelly × equity scaling — position sizes grow proportionally with portfolio value. Systematic geometric compounding embedded in the sizing engine.
