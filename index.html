How It Works

Three Layers of Edge

The system combines passive market exposure, active stock selection, and volatility harvesting into a unified engine with regime-aware controls.

1

Regime Detection

A Markov regime model classifies the market as Bull, Neutral, Caution, or Panic using VIX, credit spreads, and momentum signals. Every decision downstream adapts to the current regime.

2

ML-Filtered Execution

A LightGBM ensemble scores 11,635 tickers daily. Walk-forward cross-validation and calibrated probabilities ensure the model generalizes beyond training data.

3

Kelly-Sized Positions

Every trade is sized using the Kelly Criterion, scaled by regime confidence. Full Kelly in Bull (~16.8%), quarter-Kelly in Panic. An 8% hard cap prevents any single position from dominating.

Pro-Level Controls

What the Pros Do, Built In

Institutional risk management techniques implemented as automated controls within the trading engine.

Kelly Criterion Sizing

Mathematically optimal position sizing scaled by regime. Full Kelly (~16.8%) in Bull markets, quarter-Kelly in Panic. Hard-capped at 8% per position.

Convexity Overlay

Permanent SQQQ tail hedge — 1.5% budget in normal markets, 3.5% in stress. Costs ~1.4% annually but caps crash exposure and keeps max drawdown under 24%.

VRP Circuit Breaker

Volatility trades auto-exit if VIX breaches 40. Prevents the SVXY strategy from holding through market panics where vol-selling is catastrophic.

Trend-Following Exits

Death cross on QQQ caps allocation to 20%. Early warning signals cap at 50%. Reduces QQQ floor max drawdown from 30.9% to 17.7%.

Regime-Aware Everything

Every component adapts to the Markov regime state. Allocation, position size, entry thresholds, and stop distances all shift based on Bull/Neutral/Caution/Panic classification.

Compounding Equity

Position sizes scale with current portfolio equity via Kelly × equity. As the account grows, positions grow proportionally — geometric compounding built into the engine.

System Goals

What We Kept and What We Cut

The system was refined through iterative backtesting — removing value destroyers and adding institutional controls.

What Works

  • QQQ Floor — regime-aware passive allocation captures market drift (58% of P&L)
  • ML Stock Picking — LightGBM ensemble with walk-forward CV and calibrated probabilities
  • VRP / SVXY — short-volatility in the VIX 25–40 sweet spot, 80% win rate
  • Multi-leg Options at 8% cap — iron condors, regime spreads, gamma pins
  • Kelly Criterion sizing — mathematically optimal, regime-scaled
  • Convexity overlay — permanent SQQQ tail hedge for crash protection
  • Trend-following exits — death cross and early warning signals on QQQ

What Was Removed

  • Intraday Shorts — 28.5% win rate, lost $419K over 10 years. Removed entirely.
  • Sector Rotation (GLD/ITA) — 20.7% win rate, lost $92K from regime whipsaw. Removed.
  • Options above 8% allocation — profitable at 8%, but expanding to 15–20% turned negative due to over-sizing.
  • Simple CSPs — only complex multi-leg orders are kept. Simple CSPs didn't have consistent edge.
